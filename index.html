SQL SALES PERFORMANCE ANALYSIS

Every invoice tells a story of a customer, a purchase, a moment in time. In this project, i dive into thousands of retail transactions to uncover the hidden patterns behind everyday shopping behavior. When do people shop the most? What time of day drives the highest sales? Are weekends really busier than weekdays? Through detailed SQL analysis, invoice volumes, revenue trends, peak shopping hours, and even the timing of refunds and cancellations were unveiled. By turning raw sales data into meaningful insights, this project helps retailers better understand their business and their customers one query at a time.

View Project

Finance Insights Dashboard (ETL + SQL + Power BI)

It started with a pile of unorganized transactions — rows of credits, debits, and balances that didn’t say much at first glance. But behind those numbers was a story waiting to be told. I wanted to understand spending habits, track income flow, and uncover patterns that could lead to better financial decisions. Using Excel, I began by cleaning and organizing the raw data. Then with Python, I transformed it into a structured format and loaded it into a SQL database for easy access and analysis. Finally, I brought the data to life through an interactive Power BI dashboard, revealing trends and insights that turned a simple statement into a clear picture of financial health.

DATA CLEANING [Python Pandas]

Raw data often hides meaningful insights when it’s disorganized and unclear. In this work, I transformed a one-day supply transaction dataset where product categories and amounts were merged within single cells.
