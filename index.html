ABOUT ME

I’m Valerie Kelechukwu, a Data Analyst and Supply Chain Specialist who turns complex data into actionable business strategies. Where others see numbers, I see patterns — and where many see reports, I uncover opportunities to stop profit leakage and improve operational efficiency. My journey into data started with curiosity: understanding how patterns and numbers shape business outcomes. That curiosity evolved into hands-on experience analyzing sales, inventory, customer, and product data, particularly in FMCG, Retail, and E-commerce. I specialize in transforming messy, fragmented data into decision-ready insights. From data cleaning and modeling to visualization, predictive analysis, and forecasting, I use tools like Python, SQL, Power BI, Excel, and Machine Learning to help businesses make confident, data-driven decisions. Some of the impact I deliver includes: Identifying hidden profit leaks across supply chains Optimizing inventory to balance stockouts and excess holding costs Forecasting demand to support smarter, proactive planning Building dashboards and systems that transform complex operations into actionable insights With a First-Class B.Sc. in Computer Science, I combine technical precision with analytical creativity to help businesses grow smarter, not just bigger.
