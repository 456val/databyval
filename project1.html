Project Overview

Sales data can be complex and overwhelming, making it difficult to spot patterns or understand customer behavior. This PROJECT dives into historical sales data to uncover key insights across different timeframes‚Äîdaily, weekly, monthly, and quarterly. The aim is to identify peak sales periods, analyze time-of-day and weekday/weekend trends, and ultimately provide actionable recommendations. By transforming raw numbers into a structured narrative, this project tells the story behind the data and highlights opportunities for smarter business planning and operational efficiency.

Methodology

The analysis followed a structured approach using SQL and Excel:

  • Aggregated invoices and revenue by day, week, month, and quarter to identify trends.
  • Examined time-of-day and weekday/weekend patterns to understand customer behavior.
  • Explored seasonal and quarterly revenue fluctuations to reveal opportunities and slow periods.
  • Visualized key findings using charts and dashboards for clear storytelling.
  • Derived actionable recommendations based on observed patterns and insights.

Outcome

The analysis revealed significant trends, behavioral patterns, and operational insights that can guide business decisions and planning.
