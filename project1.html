Problem

The goal of this case study was to analyze historical sales data to uncover key trends, patterns, and behaviors. Specific questions addressed included:

  • Which days, weeks, and months had the highest or lowest sales?
  • What were the busiest hours and days of the week?
  • Are there seasonal or quarterly revenue patterns?
  • How does revenue vary between weekdays and weekends?

Action

The analysis was conducted using SQL and Excel, following a structured approach:

  • Aggregated invoices and revenue by day, week, month, and quarter
  • Analyzed time-of-day and day-of-week sales patterns
  • Examined seasonal and quarterly trends
  • Created visual dashboards to present findings
  • Derived actionable insights from statistical analysis and trends
