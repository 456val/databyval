Data Story

The company faced challenges interpreting fragmented and disorganized sales records. Key questions arose: Which products drive the most revenue? When should stock levels be adjusted? How can marketing efforts be optimized? By exploring transactional patterns, daily trends, and product performance, I transformed raw data into a compelling narrative that reveals what truly drives business growth.

Pain Points & Goals

Pain Points: Dispersed and inconsistent sales data, unclassified product categories, and limited visibility into daily and weekly revenue trends.
Goals: Build a data-driven dashboard to identify high-performing products, pinpoint peak revenue periods, and provide actionable insights for inventory planning and marketing strategies.

Methodology

The approach included data cleaning and preprocessing, exploratory data analysis to detect patterns and anomalies, and visualization using Power BI. Interactive dashboards were created to display revenue by product categories, time periods, and transaction patterns for easy interpretation by leadership.
