Methodology

The project followed a structured approach entirely in Power BI:

  • Imported and cleaned sales transaction data to ensure accuracy and consistency.
  • Structured data tables to categorize products and track revenue by day, week, and month.
  • Created calculated columns and measures to analyze revenue trends, transaction counts, and category performance.
  • Built interactive dashboards to visualize purchasing patterns, top-selling products, and temporal trends.
  • Identified key insights to inform marketing strategies, inventory management, and operational decisions.
