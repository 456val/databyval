Pharmaceutical Sales Performance Dashboard

Project Overview

Pharmaceutical companies often struggle to understand sales trends, product performance, and category contribution across multiple products and regions. Without a structured view, marketing campaigns, inventory planning, and resource allocation can be inefficient and revenue opportunities may be missed. This project focuses on transforming 6,185 transactions spanning 970 products into actionable insights to optimize sales, inventory, and marketing strategy for the July‚ÄìSeptember 2025 period.

Methodology

  • Imported and cleaned transactional data in Power BI to ensure accuracy and consistency.
  • Structured tables to track revenue, product categories, and transactions over time (day, week, month).
  • Created measures and calculated columns to monitor sales trends, top-performing products, and category contributions.
  • Built interactive dashboards to visualize revenue patterns, transaction spikes, and product performance for easy decision-making.
  • Derived actionable insights to guide marketing campaigns, inventory management, and resource allocation based on temporal and categorical trends.
