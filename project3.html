Project Overview

Methodology

Using Power BI, I applied a structured approach:

  • Analyzed revenue trends over time to identify peak periods and slow seasons.
  • Explored category performance to highlight top-selling products and identify underperforming segments.
  • Visualized weekday, weekend, and time-of-day patterns to guide operational decisions.
  • Built interactive dashboards for executives to explore trends and make data-driven decisions.
