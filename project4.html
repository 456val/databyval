British Airways Passenger Sentiment Analysis

Project Overview

Every review tells a story. This project explores how British Airways passengers truly feel about their flight experiences. By applying Natural Language Processing (NLP) to passenger reviews, sentiments are classified, emotional patterns uncovered, and key causes of satisfaction and frustration identified.

Methodology

  • Sentiment Analysis: Classified reviews into positive, neutral, and negative sentiments using Python and NLP libraries.
  • Review Length Analysis: Explored how word count relates to emotional intensity.
  • Word Cloud & Frequency Analysis: Identified top words in positive and negative reviews to understand key drivers of satisfaction or frustration.
  • Interruption Impact: Examined reviews mentioning flight delays or cancellations to assess emotional response.
  • Customer Service & Cause Analysis: Investigated how staff behavior, weather, technical, and crew issues affect sentiment.
