British Airways Passenger Sentiment Analysis

Project Overview

Airlines need clear insights into passenger experiences to improve service quality, reduce complaints, and enhance loyalty. This project uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze British Airways passenger reviews, uncovering sentiments, emotional trends, and key drivers of satisfaction or frustration.

Methodology

  • Sentiment Classification: Reviews categorized into positive, neutral, and negative sentiments using Python and NLP tools.
  • Word & Frequency Analysis: Extracted most common words to identify recurring themes in positive and negative feedback.
  • Length vs Emotion: Explored review length in relation to sentiment intensity.
  • Impact of Flight Interruptions: Analyzed reviews mentioning delays or cancellations to measure emotional impact.
  • Service & Cause Analysis: Examined staff behavior, weather, technical, and crew issues as determinants of sentiment.
